Campus Pulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Campus Pulse, a campus event management and student engagement application. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • The data we collect and why.
  • How your information is used within the app.
  • How your data is protected.
  • Your rights and choices regarding your information.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign up or log in, we may collect:

  • Name & Email Address: For user account creation and event registration.
  • Profile Picture: Used in community discussions and your profile page.
  • College ID Card: Uploaded for verification in event registrations (if required).
  • User ID: Auto-generated unique ID to identify your account.

1.2 Event Interaction Data

  • Events You Create: Details such as event title, category, venue, and timings.
  • Your Registrations: Events you sign up for.
  • Certificates: Records of events you attended.

1.3 Community Discussion Data

  • Comments & Questions: Queries posted under event discussions.
  • Replies: Messages from peers or organizers.

1.4 Technical & Usage Data

  • Login activity and device information.
  • App interactions, clicks, and event views.
  • Error logs for fixing bugs.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide core features of Campus Pulse:

  • Event Registration: Automatically fill your details when registering.
  • Event Creation: Allow organizers to create and manage events easily.
  • Certificate Generation: Provide downloadable certificates for attended events.
  • Community Support: Post questions and receive responses from peers or organizers.
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage to enhance features and performance.

3. Data Security

  • Encrypted Servers: All data stored securely with industry-standard encryption.
  • Protected Uploads: ID cards and certificates are stored securely.
  • Strict Access Control: Only authorized app administrators can access sensitive data.

4. Your Rights

  • Update Profile: Edit your personal details anytime.
  • Request Data Deletion: You can request us to delete your account permanently.
  • Control Notifications: Manage your event-related alerts.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Login: For secure authentication.
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure storage of event and user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Campus Pulse is designed for college students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Major updates will be communicated through the app.
